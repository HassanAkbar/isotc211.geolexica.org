Language: eng

Term: simple
Term ID: 411
Definition: property of a geometric object that its interior is isotropic (all points have isomorphic neighbourhoods), and hence everywhere locally isomorphic to an open subset of a Euclidean coordinate space of the appropriate dimension
notes: Note 1 to entry: This implies that no interior direct position is involved in a self-intersection of any kind.

Term ID: 411
