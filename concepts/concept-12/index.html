Term: altitude
Term ID: 12
Definition: distance of a point from a chosen reference surface along a line perpendicular to that surface
notes: Note 1 to entry: See ellipsoidal height and gravity-related height.Note 2 to entry: Height of a point outside the surface treated as positive; negative height is designated as depth.
