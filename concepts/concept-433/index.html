Language: eng

Term: strong substitutability
Term ID: 433
Definition: ability for any instance of a class that is a descendant under inheritance or realization of another class, type or interface to be used in lieu of an instance of its ancestor in any context
notes: Note 1 to entry: The weaker forms of substitutability make various restrictions on the context of the implied substitution.
