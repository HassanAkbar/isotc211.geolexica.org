Language: eng

Term: topological boundary
Term ID: 461
Definition: boundary represented by a set of oriented topological primitives of smaller topological dimension that limits the extent of a topological object
notes: Note 1 to entry: The boundary of a topological complex corresponds to the boundary of the geometric realization of the topological complex.
