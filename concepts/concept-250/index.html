Language: eng

Term: integrated positioning system
Term ID: 250
Definition: positioning system incorporating two or more positioning technologies
notes: Note 1 to entry: The measurements produced by each positioning technology in an integrated system may be any of position, motion, or attitude. There may be redundant measurements. When combined, a unified position, motion, or attitude is determined.
