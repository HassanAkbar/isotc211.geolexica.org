Language: eng

Term: attribute
Term ID: 20
Definition: feature within a classifier that describes a range of values that instances of the classifier may hold
notes: Note 1 to entry: An attribute is semantically equivalent to a composition association; however, the intent and usage is normally different. Note 2 to entry: "Feature" used in this definition is the UML meaning of the term and is not meant as defined in 4.1 of this Technical Specification.
