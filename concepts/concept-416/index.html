Term: solid
Term ID: 416
Definition: 3-dimensional geometric primitive, representing the continuous image of a region of Euclidean 3 space
notes: Note 1 to entry: A solid is realizable locally as a three parameter set of direct positions. The boundary of a solid is the set of oriented, closed surfaces that comprise the limits of the solid.
