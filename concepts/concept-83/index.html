Language: eng

Term: continuous coverage
Term ID: 83
Definition: coverage that returns different values for the same feature attribute at different direct positions within a single spatial object, temporal object, or spatiotemporal object in its domain
notes: Note 1 to entry: Although the domain of a continuous coverage is ordinarily bounded in terms of its spatial and/or temporal extent, it can be subdivided into an infinite number of direct positions.

Term ID: 83
